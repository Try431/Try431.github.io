Bio

I'm a native-born Texan, raised on books, barbecue, and boiling-hot summers. I've always had a penchant for taking pictures of the things and people around me, as well as for talking way too much, and hopefully this website will serve as a (constructive?) blending of these two.


I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and am currently a Software Engineer at AT&T/DIRECTV. When I'm not sitting behind a desk at a computer, I thoroughly enjoy most activities which take me outside, especially hiking, and am constantly thinking of new woodworking projects to pursue in my free time.
