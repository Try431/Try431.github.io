Howdy!

I'm a native-born Texan, raised on books, barbecue, and boiling-hot summers. I've always had a penchant for taking pictures of the things and people around me, as well as for talking way too much, and hopefully this website will serve as a (constructive?) blending of these two!

When I'm not sitting behind a desk at a computer, I thoroughly enjoy most activities which take me outside, especially hiking. I'm also a Bass II in the Angel City Chorale; a teacher for a local Girls Who Code club; a tutor/mentor for School On Wheels; and I volunteer with Habitat for Humanity LA -- if you've ever considered volunteering, I highly recommend checking out at least one of these great organizations!

If you'd like to take a look at some of my pictures, take a gander at my photography galleries*. Right now, I've got pictures from my trip through Iceland, my PCT/OCT hike, and a four-day trip through Inyo National Forest. I aim to get better over time, especially now that I have an actual camera...!
